About ABES

Where Engineers
are Forged

At ABES Engineering College, we ignite curiosity, knowledge, and innovation. Established over two decades ago under the Society for Educational Excellence, our institution has evolved into a dynamic center of learning in the heart of Ghaziabad.

Our unwavering commitment to excellence has earned us noteworthy NIRF rankings. Beyond engineering, our programs include M.Tech, MCA, and MBA courses — designed for the multifaceted aspirations of modern students.
